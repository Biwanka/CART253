Welcome to My Disability Experience

This website aims to share my personal experiences with various disabilities, not to create a "good" experience or simulate these challenges perfectly. It is not meant for diagnosis or sympathy but to provide insight into how I navigate life, work, and creativity while managing these conditions. Each user may experience it differently, and the goal is to help others understand the adaptations and hurdles I face daily. This website offers an exploration of my disabilities, their impact, and how they shape my creative and academic work. Choose how you’d like to proceed.

Trigger Warning:

Some of the effects on this site may be intense or disorienting, especially for users who experience visual, sensory, or neurological sensitivities. Please choose the path that feels right for you and proceed with caution. Some effects that could be triggering have buttons as you can se below to activate and deactivate them to make them more accessible. The eyes are for visual effects and the sound logo is for audio effects.
